Project
Butterfly

Join the mission of helping the women who persevere in impoverished conditions in Mumbai, India's red-light districts.
Research shows that education reduces poverty, increases opportunities. We further center on instruction that gives internal realization to each woman of their intrinsic value.
This essential instruction empowers women in Project Butterfly to face fear, anxiety and feeling trapped. Every day,women learn to read and write they are finding the restoration of hope and dignity.

What We Do

Craniofacial Surgery


Love Without Reason is committed to offering world-class, science-based medical camps which treat and screen those born with congenital craniofacial disabilities.
The medical team leaders bring expertise as heads of department in their respective fields. With extensive training and experience, these leaders share skills during the medical missions to build future medical leaders.
Medical teams and administrative leaders work together to conduct medical camps in countries with the greatest need.

Human Trafficking


Love Without Reason uses many approches to confront the growing crisis of human trafficking.
Our founder's preventative initiatives include education and awareness of connections between congenital craniofacial disabilities and natural disasters with human trafficking.
The Project Butterfly initiative focuses on providing a sustainable recovery in Mumbai, India's red-light districts.
This work provides an exit from a trafficking situation for women and children.
